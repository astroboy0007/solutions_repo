# Calculus

## Functions

### Functions:  
1. \( f(x) = x^2 \)
2. \( g(x) = \sqrt{x} \)
3. \( h(x) = \frac{1}{x} \)
4. \( j(x) = \sin(x) \)

### Calculation:  
1. For \( f(x) = x^2 \):  

\[
f(2) = 2^2 = 4
\]  

2. For \( g(x) = \sqrt{x} \):  

\[
g(2) = \sqrt{2} \approx 1.414
\]  

3. For \( h(x) = \frac{1}{x} \):  

\[
h(2) = \frac{1}{2} = 0.5
\]  

4. For \( j(x) = \sin(x) \):  

   Assuming \( x = 2 \) is in radians,  

\[
j(2) = \sin(2) \approx 0.909
\]  


### Summary:  
- \( f(2) = 4 \)
- \( g(2) \approx 1.414 \)
- \( h(2) = 0.5 \)
- \( j(2) \approx 0.909 \)
